* Basic Configuration

** Doom theme
#+begin_src emacs-lisp
(setq doom-theme 'doom-one)
#+end_src

** Font Family
#+begin_src emacs-lisp
(setq doom-font (font-spec :family "JetBrainsMono Nerd Font" :size 13 :weight 'regular))
#+end_src

** Relative Line Number
#+begin_src emacs-lisp
(setq display-line-numbers-type 'relative)
#+end_src
